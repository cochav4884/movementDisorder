Motus Collective

Supporting People Living With Movement Disorders

Motus Collective exists to help individuals and families better understand movement disorders through research-backed education, shared experiences, and community collaboration.

We believe knowledge, connection, and compassion help restore confidence in every movement.

Our Mission

Motus Collective provides educational resources, symptom awareness tools, and community collaboration opportunities for individuals affected by neurological movement disorders.

Our platform allows people to learn about available therapies, discover relief strategies others have tried, and connect with individuals experiencing similar challenges.

Relief Strategies & Daily Living Ideas

Many people living with movement disorders explore lifestyle adjustments, therapies, and supportive techniques to help manage symptoms.

Exercise

Stretching, balance training, and gentle movement exercises may help maintain coordination and flexibility.

Nutrition

Diet and nutrient balance can influence energy levels, muscle function, and neurological health.

Therapy

Physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy can support daily functioning and independence.

Symptom Awareness & Self-Reflection

Tracking symptom patterns can help individuals better communicate with medical professionals and understand changes over time.
