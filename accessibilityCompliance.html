Accessibility Compliance

Motus Collective is committed to providing an accessible experience for all visitors, including individuals with disabilities. We follow best practices and applicable guidelines to ensure our website content is usable by everyone.

Our accessibility efforts aim to support users with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive impairments.

Standards & Guidelines

  • WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) Level AA compliance
  • Keyboard navigable and screen reader-friendly design
  • Proper color contrast for readability
  • Alt text for images and icons
  • Accessible forms and interactive elements

Implementation Measures

Our website design incorporates accessible navigation, semantic HTML, responsive layouts, and ARIA attributes where needed. We continually test our website to maintain accessibility standards.

Feedback & Assistance

If you encounter any barriers while using this website, please contact us. We value your feedback and will make reasonable accommodations whenever possible.
